Biography of my favourite

Life Background

Shina was born in the late 90's by Mr & Mrs Olola Benjamin. Shina was born and bred in Plateau State. He attended Abundant Life Academy for his primary school education and Federal Government College Jos for his secondary school certificate.

Career

Shina is a Technician in Trem International Concept, a sub-contractor under Coca Cola. Holds an ND & HND in Conarina Maritime Academy. Shina has more than five years of experience in Mechanical Enginerring and specializes in AutoCAD and solid works drawings. He is a member of The Nigeria Institute of Shipping.

Christian Life

Shina was born in a christian home. He worships at The Gospel Faith Mission International, GOFAMINT. He dedicated his life to Jesus in the year 2012 and was baptized by the Redeem people mission in Lutheral Church, and ever since then he has been committed and active in a local church.
